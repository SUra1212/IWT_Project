Operational readiness and operation procedure of Outback Adventure during the pandemic period

To ensure the safety and well being of our clients, staff and our service providers, we have implemented the following safety procedures to practice once our airport is opened and when we start operating.



  • All our clients, chauffeurs and service providers are required to wear face masks.
  • Regular sanitisation of tour vehicles.
  • Regular temperature checks of our chauffeurs, service providers and clients.
  • Practice social distancing.
  • Hand sanitisation liquid bottles will be provided to all our clients at no cost.
