Minneriya National Park

The Minneriya National Park is situated in the District of Pollonnaruwa. It is within the proximity of the Minneriya Reservoir which is one of the largest man made water bodies in the country. The tank nourishes its surrounding plains throughout the year leading to high levels of biological diversity in the Minneriya National Park. The park is famous around the world as a location that welcomes one of the biggest gatherings of Asian Elephants. In addition to the elephants, the park is home to 24 species of mammals as well as a large amount of aquatic birds. A variety of faunal features and diverse habitats can also be seen in the park.
